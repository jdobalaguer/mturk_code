INSTRUCTIONS

For each pair of shapes, try to guess the button (left / right) that gives correct feedback.
It might be based on any or both of the shapes - pay attention to the angle or the length!
Respond using the LEFT and the RIGHT arrow keys in your keyboard.
You will get a CORRECT/INCORRECT feedback on every trial.
You will be guessing at first, but your brain will kick in over time.

The experiment should take about 50 - 60 min
